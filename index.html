Selected Case Studies

Rainfall–Runoff Analysis – HEC-HMS

Objective: Estimation of design inflow hydrographs for flood scenarios.

Approach: Frequency analysis, sub-basin delineation, and calibrated rainfall–runoff modelling.

Output: Design hydrographs for engineering and hydraulic simulations.

River Flood Modelling – HEC-RAS

Objective: Flood risk assessment for river channel capacity and inundation extent.

Approach: 1D/2D hydraulic modelling using observed discharge data, river cross-sections, and boundary condition analysis.

Output: Water level profiles, flood maps, and engineering interpretation for mitigation planning.

Coastal Hydrodynamics & Sediment Transport – Delft3D

Objective: Assessment of coastal processes and sediment dynamics.

Approach: Hydrodynamic and morphodynamic simulations using bathymetry, tidal forcing, and wave conditions.

Output: Flow patterns, sediment transport trends, and engineering insights for coastal management.
