I provide engineering consulting services to analyse and solve complex water-related challenges. My work covers river basins, flood-prone areas, and coastal zones with a strong focus on numerical modelling, data-driven analysis, and practical engineering solutions.

HEC-HMS Hydrological Modelling

Rainfall–runoff analysis, watershed delineation, design flood estimation, and hydrograph simulation using HEC-HMS. Suitable for flood studies, dam safety, drainage planning, and academic research.

HEC-RAS Hydraulic & Floodplain Analysis

1D/2D river hydraulics, flood inundation mapping, channel capacity assessment, and mitigation studies using HEC-RAS for engineering and regulatory purposes.

Delft3D Coastal & Sediment Modelling

Hydrodynamics, wave–current interaction, sediment transport, and shoreline response modelling using Delft3D for coastal engineering and research applications.

Academic/Research Support & Modelling Trainning

Professional support and training in hydrological and hydraulic modelling using HEC-HMS, HEC-RAS, and Delft3D, including flood analysis, water assessment, and applied engineering studies for academic and technical applications.
